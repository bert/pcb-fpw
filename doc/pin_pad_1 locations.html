Pin/pad #1 locations.

Following locations are given with regard to the top view:

  • Parts with two pins/pads: left pin/pad.
  • Parts with pins/pads on two sides: left side, top pin.
  • Parts with pins on four sides (for pin 1 on a corner): left side, top pin/pad.
  • Parts with pads on four sides (for pin 1 center): top side.
  • Pin Grid Arrays (PGA): left side, top pin.
  • Ball Grid Arrays (BGA): left side, top pad.
